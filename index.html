About Me

Hello! I'm Jersey a front-end developer with a passion for bringing creative ideas to life, whether through code, graphics, or music. My journey in coding began back in high school when I discovered my love for designing web pages. That spark grew into a full-blown passion for front-end development, where I specialize in building visually engaging and user-friendly interfaces. While my heart lies in the front end, I also have some experience with back-end technologies, allowing me to understand the full scope of web development.

Beyond coding, I'm also a graphic designer and a music producer. My interest in graphic design stems from my childhood love for drawing, which naturally evolved into digital art during my high school years. For me, design is a new dimension of storytelling, a way to express art through color, shape, and intention. Music is another major part of who I am. I started making silly rap tracks with friends in high school, which eventually led me to explore beat production seriously. Over time, I developed a deep appreciation for how music can communicate emotion and energy, it's the rhythm that drives so much of life.

Outside of tech and the arts, I’m into fitness and martial arts, which help keep me grounded and focused. I also enjoy gaming for inspiration and fun, and I find joy in cooking, it's another creative outlet where I can experiment and make something people enjoy. I’m always exploring new ways to blend my technical skills with creativity. Whether it's developing an interactive interface, designing a striking visual, or producing a beat that hits just right, I’m driven by the thrill of creating something meaningful.
