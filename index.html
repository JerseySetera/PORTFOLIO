About Me

I'm a creative technologist driven by a lifelong passion for building beautiful things—whether it's a seamless web interface, a striking visual design, or a beat that gets your head nodding. My work is a blend of code, art, and rhythm.

The Developer

My fascination with web design began in high school and has since evolved into a career in front-end development. I specialize in crafting engaging, user-friendly interfaces with a keen eye for detail. While my heart is in the front-end, I'm also versed in back-end technologies, giving me a comprehensive view of the entire development lifecycle.

The Designer

What started as a childhood love for drawing has transformed into a passion for graphic design. I see design as visual storytelling—a way to communicate ideas and emotions through color, shape, and intention. From brand identities to digital art, I love bringing concepts to life.

The Producer

Music is the rhythm that drives me. From making rap tracks with friends to seriously producing beats, I've developed a deep appreciation for the power of sound. I find joy in creating soundscapes that evoke energy and emotion, adding another layer to my creative toolkit.

Beyond the Screen

When I'm not creating, you can find me staying active with fitness and martial arts, which keep me disciplined and focused. I'm also an avid gamer—a great source of inspiration—and enjoy the creative outlet of cooking. I believe in a balanced life where discipline and creativity fuel each other.
